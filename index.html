PLACES WE CARRY

Places We Carry is a living tapestry of belonging — a map woven from the memories, feelings, and sensations we carry with us from the places that have held us.
This space invites you to share the corners of the world that feel like home, the moments that rooted you, the sensory experiences that linger, and the memories you hold dear.
Every pin is a soft imprint, a part of your journey laid beside others in this collective archive of how we carry home with us, wherever we go.

This website is developed and designed by Jolin Chen as part of her degree project, Carrying Home.
To all who entrusted your memories, sentiments, and pictures through the form, thank you ♡. Your heartful participation has helped bring the Places We Carry tapestry to life!
